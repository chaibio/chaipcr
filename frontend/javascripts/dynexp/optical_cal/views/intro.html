Open qPCR Optical Calibration

The optical calibration procedure corrects well-to-well differences in Open qPCR's optical path. The calibration should be performed whenever the lid height is adjusted, the make of PCR tubes is changed, or the Open qPCR is transported.

DURATION
~5 minutes
MATERIALS REQUIRED
4x 8-well PCR strips
200uL Water [25uL/tube]
200uL Open qPCR Fluorescein Calibration Solution [25uL/tube]
*It is important to pipette the calibration solution from the same stock tube. If the solution is pipetted from different stock tubes, the stock solution should first be mixed.
